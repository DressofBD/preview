Minneapolis Airport Car Service : About Us

Airport Limo Minneapolis has been serving Clients in Minneapolis – Saint Paul Twin Cities for over 8 years. Airport Limo Minneapolis have proudly served our passengers with a smile and with utmost courtesy. We always try to improve our customers service in every best way we can. Over the years, we have served thousands of passengers including Residents & Families of twin cities & Suburban Towns, Corporates, Airport Travelers, To Mayo Clinic Rochester and visitors who fly in & out of Minneapolis – Saint Paul International Airport for leisure, personal and Business Trips, School Kids, Senior Citizens and all walk of life.

We at Minneapolis Airport Car Service, provide comfortable and safe transport services to all its valuable customers, on-time, every-time. The goal of Minneapolis Airport Car Service is to achieve the highest standards in customer service quality and 100% customer satisfaction.
