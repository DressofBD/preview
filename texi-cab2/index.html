About Us

Minneapolis Car Service locally owned and Minneapolis based Car Service provider since past six years. All of our vehicles are well maintained and fully licensed and insured. We offer 24 hours a day, 7 days a week service.

Minneapolis Car Service provides a wide variety of Car services for Point-to-point Rides, Airport Transfers, Ride to Mayo Clinic to Rochester MN, for Sporting Events, Shopping Drives, National Parks, Pickup & Drop at Minnesota Zoo, Treasure Island and Dinner & Dates from Minneapolis - Saint Paul Twin Cities and the suburban areas. We also offer a complete range of car services including Town Car Sedans, Mercedes s550, Passenger Vans and SUVs at very competitive rates.
