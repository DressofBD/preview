About us

Welcome to Fulham Taxis, Fulham's leading cab-company provide a reliable, safe and professional service.

Our fares are always low and fixed, carefully calculated using the latest technology and worked out according to distance and time of the route. We make practical and effective use of the latest state-of-the-art booking and data dispatching technology, providing satellite navigation in all vehicles and GPRS tracking.

With our trained and professional drivers our aim is to get our valued customers to their destinations safe and on time, 24 hours a day. We pick up and drop off anywhere in London and UK.

Booking for an airport transfer is simple-call us or book online and as with all our minicab services, you will receive a text message when your car is on its way and another when it is waiting outside.

We at Fulham Taxis understand your transport needs, so call us today for taxis or package courier services in Greater London.

We have a superior fleet of minicabs which include Mercedes Benz, people carriers and minibuses. All our vehicles are kept immaculately clean with our drivers being very helpful and courteous. We welcome account customers and contract work and are licensed and regulated by the Public Carriage Office (PCO).
