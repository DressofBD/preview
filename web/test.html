Just as you have a lens in your glasses to focus there is a lens in the eye to focus. When you are young this changes shape to bring things into focus. As you age it hardens and stops changing shape. As it hardens reading glasses are subsequently required. This hardening is called presbyopia.

Problems occur when the lens loses its clarity and becomes opaque. This causes blurring that can not be corrected with glasses. This is known as a cataract. Cataract surgery is the commonest operation in the country.
