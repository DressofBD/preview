Dover Royal Taxis

At Dover Royal Taxi, we focus on one thing: offering top-notch Dover taxi booking and private transfer service. Providing customers with an unforgettable transfer experience isn’t just another flashy slogan; it’s the staple of what our dedicated personnel and fully licensed & certified drivers strive to deliver every day. We go above and beyond to offer a service that ensures that you’re 100% satisfied, but doesn’t break the bank.

Who We Are

Dover Royal Taxis is a Dover-based local transport company that has been in the taxis and private transfer business for over 10 years. Our central office is situated just 15 minutes from Dover cruise terminals, something that gives us an added edge over our competition. We have a team of exceptionally experienced & fully licensed drivers, devoted personnel, and customer care specialists.

We’re your Dover taxi specialist, and we do it all. You can count on us to provide you with transfers to/from all Dover’s nearby airports, seaports, train stations, sports venues, local destinations, weddings, corporate functions, private events, and so forth. Our professionals are trained and reliable, ensuring that you get value for your money each and every time.

We take great pride in being one of a handful of Dove taxi companies that boast of bespoke taxis. Whether you need wheelchair taxis or taxi for pets to Calais, we have got you covered! Moreover, our state-of-the-art fleet comprises all types of vehicles - all kept immaculately clean - to fit your needs and budget.

We operate 24/7, 365 days a year so that you can rely on us to be there when you need us. But, what truly makes us stand out in a sea of competition are our sterling drivers who are well-presented, courteous, and ready to help.
