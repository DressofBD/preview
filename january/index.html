  • Campaign Eligibility:
    1. This Campaign is open to the assured or life assured of the matured policy who purchased new policy of any regular premium (RP) plan and submitted the proposal to PAMB within the promotion period stated in clause 2(a) whereby such policy has already been captured within the captured period stated in clause 2(b) (“Eligible Customer”).
    2. A policy is an Eligible Policy where, the Eligible Customer:
      i. Submits the new proposal form through PRUWay Plus and the policy must be in-force at all times with no partial withdrawal performed on the policy; and
      ii. All premium payments for the policy must be up-to-date at all times at the time of crediting. If policy with premium payments that remain outstanding upon the expiry of the 30-day grace period, Eligible Customer will be disqualified from participating in this Campaign.
    3. To participate in this Campaign and to receive the Rebate (as hereinafter defined), the Eligible Customer must provide PAMB with his/her valid bank account details, which include the bank account name and bank account number (“Bank Account Details”). The Bank Account Details must be current, valid and subsisting of which the Bank Account Details must be captured within PAMB’s system upon proposal submission.

    1. Each Eligible Policy will be entitled to a 10% Premium Rebate (“Rebate”) based on the annual premium amount of the Eligible Policy. PRUSaver/PRUSaver Kid Premium, Single Premium Top-Up are excluded from the calculation of Rebate.
    2. Once the Eligible Customer fulfils the Campaign Eligibility as per clause 3, the Rebate will be credited into the Eligible Customer’s bank account 3 months after the Eligible Policy is captured. SMS will be sent to the Eligible Customer’s registered mobile number with PAMB to inform the Eligible Customer that the Rebate has been credited into the Eligible Customer’s Bank Account. The Cashback will be forfeited in the event of:

      iii. any other reasons that caused unsuccessful Campaign Rewards crediting.
